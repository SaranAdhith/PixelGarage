Briefing

Good To Know
Before We Start.

The things founders usually ask us first — answered upfront.

What kinds of projects do you take on?

iOS & Android apps, custom websites, and the backends and APIs behind them. We can build one piece or the whole product end to end.

Do you build from scratch or use templates?

Everything is designed and coded from scratch to fit your brand and needs — no off-the-shelf templates.

Who owns the code and product?

You do. We hand over full ownership of the code, backend and accounts at launch.

Do you support the product after launch?

Yes — we stay on for bug fixes, updates and iterations to keep your product healthy as it grows.

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects ship in 4–12 weeks depending on scope. After scoping, we share a clear timeline with milestones so you always know what's coming next.

How much does a project cost?

Pricing depends on scope and complexity. We quote upfront with no surprises — you'll know the full cost and payment milestones before we start building.

Can you work with our existing team or codebase?

Absolutely. We can build alongside your in-house team, pick up an existing codebase, or take a project the full way on our own — whatever fits your setup.

How do we get started?

Tell us about your idea using the form below or email us directly. We'll reply, scope the work, and share clear milestones.
